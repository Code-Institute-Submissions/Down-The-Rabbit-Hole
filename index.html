This is me....

I am a self taught artist and I specialise in mixed media. I love experimenting with new unique mediums as well as the old classics. With a passion for Japanese and Korean inspired art, I like to create personalised paintings using these unique styles. I also specialise in cartoon animals and characters which I can transfer to many types of material. I am currently studying Art at the Open University so I am always discovering new techniques.
